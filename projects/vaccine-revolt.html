Vaccine Revolt is a board game that happens in 1904 during The Pereira Passos Reform, an important moment in Rio de Janeiro's history. The game brings historic elements, buildings and monuments from the city's downtown closer to the player.

GAME OBJECTIVE

Vaccinate as many people as possible!

VACCINE CARDS
OPINION ON VACCINES

Favorable - they want to be vaccinated!

Neutrals - their opinion varies by the team.

Contrary - they don't want a vaccine!

There are two teams in the game:

Peacemakers

Earn points by vaccinating favorable or neutral.

Rebellious

Earn points by vaccinating contrary or neutral.

The tension Meter is used to count the points earned on each vaccine and define the winning team

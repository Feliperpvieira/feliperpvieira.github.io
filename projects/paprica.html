Páprica is a box filled with fresh ingredients delivered with in-depth recipes that are well written and easy to follow, allowing people from all levels of experience to start exploring the kitchen.

It's all done in 4 simple steps:

  • Choose

    Single purchase or in a subscription plan, you pick whatever fits the best your needs.

  • Delivery

    Every two weeks we deliver to you a box with fresh products, measured for a two-person meal.

  • Cooking

    Are you in a hurry? In the mood for a challenge? There are many recipe options, you can pick the best for you.

  • Flavor

    Now it's time to taste! Recipes are easy to cook and use ingredients that you can buy anywhere.

Recipes delivered together with the ingredients, plus the step-by-step video guide.
